Clinical Care Flowchart

Algorithm

  1. Patient arrives
  2. Show online appointment/registration
  3. Check for COVID symptoms
  4. Proceed to Patient Reception and Processing
  5. Check old record / Fill-up new form
  6. Record current health condition
  7. Check available doctor
  8. Assign pattient to doctor
  9. Get assessed by doctor
  10. Check if patient needs laboratory or medication
  11. Process billing and payment
  12. Patient leaves

Pseudocode

  • BEGIN
  • CHECK for online appointment
  • REPEAT-UNTIL patient has registered online
  • IF with COVID symptoms
  • Cannot accommodate patient
  • Patient leaves
  • END
  • ELSE proceed to Patient Reception & Processing
  • ENDIF
  • IF new patient
  • INPUT fill-up patient form
  • ELSE OUTPUT retrieve existing patient form
  • ENDIF
  • Record current health conditions
  • WHILE no available doctor
  • Wait for doctor
  • ENDWHILE
  • Assign patient to doctor
  • Get assessed by doctor
  • IF patient needs laboratory
  • Proceed to examination area
  • WHILE results are not available
  • Wait for results
  • ENDWHILE
  • Get results
  • Get assessed by doctor
  • IF patient needs medication
  • Get prescription
  • ELSE process billing & payment
  • ENDIF
  • Pay final amount
  • END
  • ELSE IF patient needs medication
  • Get prescription
  • Process billing & payment
  • Pay final amount
  • ELSE process billing & payment
  • Pay final amount
  • ENDIF
  • END
